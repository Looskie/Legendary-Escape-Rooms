Who are we?

Click the Nav Bar on the top right or double click!

Our company, Legendary Escape Rooms all started 5 years ago in Woodstock, Naives. The women who built this company, Amelia Blake, wanted to make escape rooms that people of all ages would enjoy. She came up with the idea to make a company called “Legendary Escape Rooms”, the rooms would be located all around the country. Blake then started coming up with ideas for the rooms; they would be colorful and they wouldn't feature scary, horror type things like other escape rooms she had visited, she wanted people of all ages to enjoy them. Now, five years later, we have Legendary Escape Rooms located all over the country. We strive to excel in everything we do, from making the escape rooms a comfortable, safe environment, as well as making other activities such as game rooms and eating areas in our locations.
